What this build does

The checker mirrors the product flow you described: one file, no setup, three screens, transparent scoring, and clear disclosures about its limitations.

AI-style article brief

The checker extracts the likely headline, topic, tone, and keywords before the score is finalized so users understand what the article appears to claim.

Multi-module scoring

Five modules adjust a neutral score of 50: source-domain credibility, cross-source coverage, clickbait language, text quality, and fact indicators.

Only matched sources shown up front

The main report lists only the outlets that appear to match. A deeper panel explains why other sources were not considered a match.

Flagged language in context

Clickbait triggers are highlighted in red, vague attribution in amber, and fact-supporting signals like dates and explicit attribution in green.
